Based on a single self-developed 3D camera, achieved panoramic 3D reconstruction through multi-angle movement.

- Used VSLAM technology for multi-view pose estimation and point cloud registration
- Used Lie algebra for extrinsic representation, effectively eliminating accumulated errors in point cloud stitching through global nonlinear optimization
