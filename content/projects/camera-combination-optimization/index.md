For multi-camera panoramic measurement scenarios using numerous cameras in the self-developed vibration digital twin system, achieved weighted camera combination optimization for 3D estimation by evaluating camera measurement reliability.

- Designed projection-matrix-based camera evaluation, solving the inapplicability of camera combination in large-scale scenarios due to calibration difficulty
- First to introduce weighted camera combination optimization in multi-camera vibration measurement, improving measurement information utilization compared to pairing
- Resolved measurement bias at selection region boundaries in camera pairing optimization, effectively improving measurement smoothness
